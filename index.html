Verge is a secure and anonymous cryptocurrency,

Why Verge?

Verge improves upon the original Bitcoin blockchain by using multiple anonynmity-centric networks such as Tor and i2p. It utilizes multi-algorithm mining support to improve security and provide equal distribution of coins to miners. Simply put, Verge is a decentralized currency designed for users who value privacy.
